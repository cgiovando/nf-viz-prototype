Green and blue water embedded in food trade

The water that grew the food a country imports or exports - split between rain-fed (green) and irrigation (blue). 2019, million m³.

What to notice

  • USA, Brazil, Argentina are the world's largest green water exporters - feed and grain shipped abroad represents enormous quantities of rain-fed soil moisture.
  • China imports massive quantities of green water via food and feed - a deliberate strategy that effectively rents farmland (and rainfall) abroad.
  • Pakistan, India are large blue water exporters - their agricultural exports embed enormous quantities of irrigation water from already-stressed aquifers and rivers.
  • Japan, Germany, Korea are major net green water importers - their food security strategy is built around imported calories.
