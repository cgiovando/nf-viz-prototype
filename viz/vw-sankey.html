Sectoral virtual water flows

Where a country's virtual water enters or leaves the economy by sector. Drawn from the GTAP MRIO sectoral dataset (Taheripour and Chepeliev) - referenced in the report but not previously visualized.

Source: Taheripour and Chepeliev GTAP MRIO sectoral virtual water dataset, 2019 baseline. Top sectors shown; smaller sectors aggregated as "Other sectors" where applicable.
