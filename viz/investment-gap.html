Repurposing perspective

The funding needed to transform agricultural water management is between 5% (low-end need vs subsidies, $24B / $490B) and 14% (high-end need vs subsidies, $70B / $490B) of what governments already spend each year on agricultural subsidies. The financing argument is not "find new money" - it is spend existing money smarter.
